About Us

Project Story
Voice + Emotion Detection Chatbot

Most chatbots only look at plain text and ignore how the user actually feels. VoiceMood AI is built to fix that by combining speech recognition and emotion analysis to make responses feel more human and supportive.

  • Understands both what you say (text) and how you say it (tone, pitch, emotion).
  • Can soften responses when users sound sad or stressed, and celebrate when they sound happy.
  • Ideal for support apps, study helpers, mental health check-ins, or customer service bots.
Tech Breakdown
Phase-wise execution plan
  • 1.Build the UI with mic button and this scrolling landing page for a clean demo.
  • 2.Capture audio in the browser and send to Flask backend.
  • 3.Use Whisper or similar API to convert speech to text.
  • 4.Extract features (pitch, MFCC, energy) and run an emotion classifier trained on datasets like RAVDESS.
  • 5.Combine text + emotion and send back an empathetic reply from your chatbot logic.
